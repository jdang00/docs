## What LearnTerms is

LearnTerms is a collaborative study platform built around the way a cohort actually learns together.

At its core, LearnTerms is three things:

1. A shared study space for your cohort
2. An AI-assisted question creation system for contributors and admins
3. A growing question bank organized by semester, class, and module

## Join your cohort

To get into the right shared workspace, you need your cohort join code.

1. Open the LearnTerms join flow.
2. Enter the code your cohort was given.
3. Confirm the class details before joining.

Once you join, LearnTerms can show you the classes, modules, and study material tied to that cohort.

## How the product is organized

LearnTerms is intentionally opinionated about structure:

- A `cohort` groups the people studying together
- A `class` represents a course
- A `module` breaks a class into study units
- A `question` belongs to the module you practice

That structure keeps studying focused. You do not need to build your own system before you begin.

## What you see first

After onboarding, most users work from a dashboard that centers on:

- Current classes
- Module progress
- Quick access to studying
- Admin tools, if your role allows them
